## User Authentication & Rewards

- Users are required to log in before placing a pizza order. This ensures:
  - Purchase history can be saved and accessed in their account.
  - Eligibility for our loyalty rewards program (e.g., earn points per order).
  - Personalized recommendations and order preferences.
- Why this is beneficial for users?
  - In a standard situation, user will need the order recipt. Keeping the recipt is a safe way to handle any issues that might happen. 

- Guest checkout may be offered in the future, but full features (like order tracking and rewards) require login.

- Users can sign in via Google, LinkedIn, or create a custom PizzaNow account.


## Customer Service & Issue Resolution

### Order Issues / Exchanges
If you received the wrong order, contact us within **30 minutes** of delivery.  
Options:
- Replacement at no charge
- Full refund if resolution isn’t possible quickly

### Cancellations
- Cancel within **5 minutes** of placing order
- After that, partial refunds may apply

### Complaints
We take every complaint seriously.  
Send to:
- Email: support@pizzanow.com
- Phone: (604) 123-4567  
Include:
- Order ID
- Delivery time
- Description of issue

### Exchanges
We **do not accept food returns** due to safety.  
However, exchanges may be issued for:
- Quality issues
- Wrong item delivered
- Allergy label mismatch

### Allergy Notice
All pizzas are prepared in the same kitchen.  
While vegan and gluten-sensitive options are offered, **cross-contamination is possible**.
